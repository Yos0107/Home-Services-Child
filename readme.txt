== Description ==

Home Services is a feature-rich WordPress theme devoted to home service businesses like construction, HVAC, repair and maintenance, handyman, maintenance and renovation, plumbing, gardening and landscaping, renovation and remodeling, moving and storage, applliance repair and installation, plumber service, home-based fitness service, personal concierge, animal care, mobile repair, pressure washing, electrical, carpentry, flooring and hardwood, painting and staining, cleaning and maid, and such other local businesses. Built with a focus on speed and features specific to local businesses, service contractors, home improvement professionals, local business owners, it is responsive, supports cross-browser compatibility, SEO optimized, accessibility-ready, fully customizable and comes with a modern and functional design with pre-made inner page layouts.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's alleythemes.zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
